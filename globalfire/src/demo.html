About Us

Global Fire are specialists in fire safety and protection

Passive fire protection is the containment of fire within designated areas.

We work closely with suppliers to ensure we’re always up-to-date with new products that offer cost savings for clients.

Quality Workmanship

Over the years we have developed and worked hard to become a trusted name amongst the construction industry not just including contractors but to council, consultants, and fire engineers.

Services

Some of the many services that we offer

Passive Fire Stopping

Services passing through walls and floors can compromise the integrity of the fire cell. Services passing through these cells will need to be adequately fire rated to ensure the safety of building occupants.

Intumescent Painting

Structural steel may need to be fire rated in order to maintain their structural integrity in the event of a fire.

Fire Doors

Fire doors are critical for the containment of fire within buildings.

Third Party Inspection

We can assess projects and provide recommendations to ensure your project is compliant with the New Zealand building code.

Specialist Consultation

At any stage of the project we can provide expert consultation to provide you with cost saving recommendations and ensure that the passive fire systems are compliant.
