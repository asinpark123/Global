About Us

Welcome to Global Active Fire, Auckland's leading fire protection specialist. We specialise in:

  • Automatic Fire Sprinkler Systems
  • Fire alarm Systems
  • Building Hydrant systems
  • Hand-held firefighting equipment
  • And more

At Global Active Fire, we prioritize the safety of people above all else. Our experienced team works closely with clients to deliver tailored fire protection solutions that not only safeguard property but also ensure the safety of occupants.

From commercial buildings to industrial facilities, we are dedicated to providing reliable and efficient fire protection systems that give you peace of mind. Whether it's through the installation of state-of-the-art equipment or ongoing maintenance to keep your systems running smoothly, we are committed to keeping your premises and the people within them safe from the threat of fire.

Services

Our comprehensive services include:

Design

At Global Active Fire, our design services encompass the creation of customized fire protection systems tailored to the unique needs of each client. Utilizing advanced design software and industry best practices, our experienced designers develop comprehensive plans that ensure optimal coverage, compliance with regulations, and integration with existing building systems. Our goal is to create solutions that are both effective and efficient, safeguarding lives and properties with precision.

Supply

We provide a full range of high-quality fire protection products sourced from leading manufacturers around the world. Our supply services include the procurement of fire detection systems, suppression systems, alarms, and related equipment. By partnering with trusted suppliers, we ensure that every component meets stringent quality and safety standards, offering reliability and peace of mind to our clients.

Installation

Our installation services are executed by a team of skilled technicians who bring extensive experience to each project. We handle the complete installation process, ensuring that all systems are set up correctly and function as intended. Adhering to strict safety protocols and industry standards, we guarantee a seamless installation that minimises disruption to your operations while maximising protection.

Testing

Global Active Fire conducts thorough testing of all fire protection systems to verify their functionality and effectiveness. Our testing procedures include detailed inspections and performance evaluations, identifying any potential issues before they become critical. We ensure that every system is fully operational and compliant with all regulatory requirements, providing you with confidence in your fire protection measures.

Commissioning

Commissioning is a critical phase where we bring your fire protection systems into active service. Our team oversees the final checks and adjustments, ensuring that all components are correctly integrated and operating at peak efficiency. We provide comprehensive documentation and training for your staff, ensuring they understand the system's capabilities and procedures for optimal use.

Maintenance

Ongoing maintenance is essential to ensure the long-term reliability and effectiveness of your fire protection systems. Our maintenance services include regular inspections, testing, and servicing to keep your systems in top condition. We offer tailored maintenance plans that address the specific needs of your facility, ensuring compliance with regulations and providing continuous protection.
